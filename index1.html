ABOUT ME

I am currently pursuing my PhD in Computer Science and Engineering at The Pennsylvania State University. I received my B. Tech in Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering from National Institute of Technology, Rourkela.

CURRENT RESEARCH

I am a member of High Performance Computing Lab where I work with my advisors Dr. Chita R. Das. and Dr. Mahmut T. Kandemir. I also collaborate with Dr. Onur Mutlu. My current research focuses on designing high performance energy-efficient heterogeneous GPU architectures.
