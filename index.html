ABOUT ME

I am currently pursuing my PhD in Computer Science and Engineering at The Pennsylvania State University. I am a member of High Performance Computing Lab where I work with my advisor Dr. Chita R. Das. I also collaborate with Dr. Mahmut T. Kandemir in the Microsystems Design Lab. I received my B. Tech in Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering from National Institute of Technology, Rourkela.

After an awesome summer at Austin,TX working with amazing people at AMD Research, I am back at Graduate School to resume my Ph.D.!

RESEARCH INTERESTS

Broadly, my research interests lie in the area of computer architecture. Currently, I am working on designing high performance energy-efficient heterogeneous GPU architectures.
